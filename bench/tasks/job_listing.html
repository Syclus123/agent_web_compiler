Senior Full-Stack Engineer

Engineering — Platform Team San Francisco, CA (Hybrid — 3 days in office) Full-time Posted: June 1, 2025
Compensation: $180,000 – $240,000 base + equity + benefits
Apply Now

About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Full-Stack Engineer to join our Platform Team and help build the next generation of our developer tools platform. You will work on systems that serve over 2 million developers daily, handling 500 million API requests per day with 99.99% uptime requirements.

This role offers the opportunity to work on technically challenging problems at scale, including real-time collaboration features, distributed build systems, and developer-facing APIs. You will report to the Engineering Manager and work closely with Product, Design, and Infrastructure teams.

Requirements

Must Have

  • 7+ years of professional software engineering experience
  • Strong proficiency in TypeScript and Python
  • Experience building and operating production web applications at scale (1M+ users)
  • Deep understanding of React or similar frontend frameworks
  • Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred) and caching systems (Redis)
  • Familiarity with cloud infrastructure (AWS or GCP)
  • Strong understanding of API design (REST and GraphQL)
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Nice to Have

  • Experience with real-time systems (WebSockets, CRDTs)
  • Contributions to open-source projects
  • Experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration
  • Background in developer tools or platform engineering
  • Experience mentoring junior engineers

What You'll Do

  • Design, implement, and maintain features across the full stack (React, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL)
  • Lead technical design for new platform capabilities serving millions of developers
  • Improve system reliability, performance, and observability
  • Collaborate with Product and Design to define and refine requirements
  • Mentor engineers, conduct code reviews, and raise the engineering bar
  • Participate in on-call rotation (typically 1 week per quarter)
  • Contribute to engineering culture through tech talks, documentation, and process improvements

Benefits and Perks

  • Competitive equity package with 4-year vesting
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance (100% employee, 80% dependents)
  • 401(k) with 4% company match
  • $5,000 annual learning and development budget
  • Flexible PTO (minimum 15 days encouraged)
  • 16 weeks paid parental leave
  • Monthly wellness stipend ($150/month)
  • Home office setup allowance ($2,500 one-time)

About TechCorp

TechCorp is a developer tools company founded in 2018, now serving over 2 million developers at 150,000 organizations worldwide. We recently completed a $200 million Series D at a $3.5 billion valuation. Our mission is to make software development 10x more productive.

We are a team of 450 people across San Francisco, London, and Tokyo. We value technical excellence, user empathy, and transparent communication.
